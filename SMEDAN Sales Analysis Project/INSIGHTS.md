# Key Insights – SMEDAN Sales Analysis

## 1. High-Value Transactions
Several sales transactions exceeded ₦30,000,000, indicating the presence of large-volume or premium customers. These high-value sales are critical revenue drivers and should be prioritized for retention.

---

## 2. Customer Type Performance
Analysis shows that **Hotels and Restaurants** consistently generate higher total sales values compared to Retail customers.  
This suggests that bulk purchasing by hospitality businesses significantly impacts revenue.

**Business Recommendation:**  
Strengthen partnerships and loyalty programs for Hotel and Restaurant clients.

---

## 3. Product Contribution
Certain products appear repeatedly in high-value transactions, indicating strong market demand.  
Products with higher quantities sold also contribute disproportionately to total revenue.

**Business Recommendation:**  
Focus inventory planning and promotions on top-performing products.

---

## 4. Salesperson Effectiveness
Sales performance varies by salesperson and year. Some salespeople consistently close higher-value deals, especially during specific periods.

**Business Recommendation:**  
Use top performers as benchmarks for training and incentive programs.

---

## 5. Sales Trend Over Time
Yearly aggregation reveals fluctuations in sales volume and value, highlighting growth periods and slower sales years.

**Business Recommendation:**  
Align marketing campaigns and supply planning with high-performing years and seasons.

---

## 6. Overall Sales Summary
- Total revenue shows strong dependence on a small number of high-value transactions.
- Average sales value masks large variability between customers.
- Maximum sales values far exceed the minimum, confirming uneven revenue distribution.
