##  Project Overview
This project analyzes hospital encounter data using **MySQL** to
uncover trends in patient visits encounter types, healthcare costs, payer coverage,
and patient readmission behavior.
The goal is to generate actionable
insights that can support hospital operations, cost management, and patient care improvement.

The analysis focuses on encounter volume over time, 
cost distribution, insurance coverage, procedure frequency, and patient readmissions.

---

##  Objectives

### Objective 1: Encounters Overview
- Analyze total encounters by year
- Identify encounter class distribution (ambulatory, outpatient, inpatient, emergency, etc.)
- Compare encounters lasting over 24 hours vs under 24 hours

### Objective 2: Cost & Coverage Insights
- Measure encounters with zero payer coverage
- Identify most frequent procedures and their average costs
- Identify procedures with the highest average base cost
- Analyze average total claim cost by payer

### Objective 3: Patient Behavior Analysis
- Track unique patient admissions by quarter
- Identify patients readmitted within 30 days
- Highlight patients with the highest readmission rates
